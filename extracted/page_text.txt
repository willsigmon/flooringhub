Hardwood Flooring
Elevate your space with the timeless beauty and durability of
hardwood flooring
. Known for its natural elegance and long-lasting performance, hardwood adds warmth and sophistication to any room—residential or commercial. Available in a wide range of wood species, finishes, and plank sizes, our hardwood flooring options offer unmatched versatility to complement both classic and contemporary designs.
Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) Flooring
Achieve the perfect balance of style, durability, and practicality with Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) flooring. Designed to mimic the natural look of hardwood while offering enhanced resistance to moisture, scratches, and wear, LVP is a smart and stylish flooring solution for all spaces.
Laminate Flooring
Stylish, durable, and cost-effective—laminate flooring offers the perfect solution for customers seeking the look of hardwood or stone without the high price tag. Engineered with multiple layers for strength and stability, laminate is a versatile option that works beautifully in both residential and commercial environments.
Floor Leveling
Achieve a flawless foundation with our full pour leveling services. Using high-performance self-leveling compounds, we create a perfectly smooth and even subfloor—essential for the proper installation and long-term performance of your new flooring. Ideal for both residential and commercial projects, our process ensures your floor looks great and performs even better.
Carpeted Flooring
Add comfort, warmth, and style to any space with our premium carpeting solutions. Our carpets combine softness underfoot with durability and stain resistance. We offer a wide selection of materials, textures, and colors to match any interior design— from plush, luxurious piles to low-profile, easy-to-clean options.
Additional Services
At Flooring Hub, we go beyond standard flooring installation. We proudly offer comprehensive services designed to ensure your entire flooring project is seamless, efficient, and beautifully finished. From expert floor prep to custom finishing touches, our additional services provide everything you need to create lasting, high-performance floors.
ABOUT FLOORING HUB
Flooring Hub
is a locally owned and operated flooring company proudly serving the Triangle and surrounding communities. Founded and led by Tom Smith, a craftsman with over 25 years of hands-on experience, Flooring Hub stands apart from large franchises by delivering personal service grounded in integrity, precision, and trust.
Tom’s passion for flooring goes beyond installation—it’s about creating a lasting foundation for homes his clients love. He personally partners with each customer, guiding them through every step, from selecting the perfect materials to ensuring flawless completion. Specializing in hardwood, laminate, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), carpet, and custom inlays, Flooring Hub also offers expert floor leveling to guarantee a smooth, professional finish every time.
Every project follows strict national standards and manufacturer guidelines, ensuring quality workmanship, valid warranties, and durable results. But what truly makes Flooring Hub unique is the direct relationship clients have with Tom—never rotating crews or pushy salespeople—ensuring transparency, quality service, and above all else, no shortcuts.
By collaborating closely with top-tier suppliers and skilled artisans who share the same commitment to excellence, Flooring Hub promises outcomes that consistently exceed expectations. For homeowners who want flooring done once, done right, and with care that lasts a lifetime, Flooring Hub delivers more than just a floor—it builds trust and a foundation for your home’s future.

FLOORING HUB EXCLUSIVES
Flooring Hub offers a variety of exclusive products for our projects:
Uzin Leveling Products
Precision. Performance. Perfectly Level Floors.
UZIN leveling compounds are high-performance solutions designed to create smooth, even subfloors for all types of floor coverings. Whether you’re preparing concrete, screed, or wooden substrates, UZIN offers a wide range of self-leveling and patching compounds tailored to meet the demands of commercial, residential, and industrial projects.
Carlisle Wide Plank Flooring
Craftsmanship You Can Walk On
Carlisle Wide Plank Flooring offers high-end, handcrafted hardwood floors that bring timeless beauty and character to any space. Renowned for its meticulous attention to detail, sustainable sourcing, and bespoke customization, Carlisle transforms natural wood into extraordinary flooring that’s as enduring as it is elegant.
Sheoga Hardwood
Timeless Beauty. Precision Craftsmanship. American-Made.
Sheoga Hardwood Flooring is synonymous with premium quality, American craftsmanship, and enduring natural beauty. Milled in the heart of Amish country in Ohio, Sheoga floors are handcrafted with exceptional attention to detail using sustainably sourced hardwoods. Whether you’re designing a traditional home or a contemporary space, Sheoga delivers a floor that’s as durable as it is distinctive.
