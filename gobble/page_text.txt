About Flooring Hub
Meet Tom Smith.
Owner & Master Craftsman
Tom's journey started with nothing more than tools in his truck and an obsession with doing the job right. For over 25 years, he has been perfecting the art of flooring -- not just installing hardwoods, laminate, LVP, and carpet, but creating spaces that transform how people feel about their homes.
This is not a franchise, and it's not about volume. It's personal. Every project carries Tom's personal signature -- because his name, reputation, and pride are on the line. He refuses to cut corners, oversell, or use installation methods that void the manufacturer's warranty.
You won't deal with a rotating crew or a sales rep. You'll deal directly with Tom -- on-site, accountable, and committed to earning your trust.

FAQ
Common questions, straight answers.
What types of flooring does Flooring Hub specialize in?
Hardwood, laminate, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), carpeted flooring, custom inlays, and more. We handle the full spectrum of residential and commercial flooring.
Do you offer floor leveling?
Yes. We provide full-pour leveling services using high-performance self-leveling compounds as part of our prep services.

Why is professional installation important?
Professional installation ensures quality, longevity, and a flawless finish. We follow strict national standards and manufacturer guidelines to protect your warranty and investment.
Can you handle complex flooring projects?
Absolutely. We specialize in custom trim, staircases, moisture mitigation, and challenging layouts. No project is too complex for our team.
